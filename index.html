    IDS System

    The IDS vision is to enhance the diving experience for all divers. The vision is to design a diving buddy (a submersible self-automated device) that is equipped with several cameras, sensors, and communications devices that will help to collect data while diving. This will give divers a personal assistant which will assess dives, collect and analyze data, and transmit data automatically to centralized servers, and or personal computers.
